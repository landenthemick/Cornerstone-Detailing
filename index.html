Driveway Cleaning

First a biodegradable chemical solution is applied to the concrete and let sit. Next we go over the concrete slowly using a high pressure circular surface cleaner. Once this is done, we use a high pressure wand to touch up any missed areas or corners. Finally, we use a water broom to clean up the dirt and debris lifted from the concrete. We offer the option to use a concrete sealer on the driveway which will help the driveway keep its clean look for longer as well as prevent cracks for up to 2 years.

Patio Cleaning

The most common materials used in patios are, brick, concrete, wood, tile and slate. The process for cleaning brick, concrete, and slate are the same. A solution is placed on the patio and is washed down using a high pressure wand. Wood patios are slightly more complicated, this is because if the pressure used is too high it could be harmful to the wood. What we do to prevent this is use a low pressure tip at a 40-60 degree angle. Then the deck/patio is washed at a low psi to remove dirt and grime.

Sidewalk Cleaning

Sidewalks visually complement the property. It is our intention to maintain a high-quality sidewalk image through the process of removing accumulated dirt, mold, mildew, algae, alongside other unwanted substances. Concrete surfaces are constantly subject to various environmental factors that may influence appearance or quality. The sidewalk process involves the use of biodegradable chemicals that combats the accumulated substances. Next, the high-pressure wand is applied to touch up the overall look and get the dirt out. A water broom is utilized to eliminate the lingering debris and dirt. The option for sidewalk concrete sealing is available upon request.

Fence Cleaning

Fence cleaning involves locating areas that need to be washed down. Then a low pressure tip is installed on the pressure washer and the fence is carefully cleaned.
